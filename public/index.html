About SABIC

SABIC is the official student-governing body of Bukhari Da’wa College. We stand as the voice of the students, dedicated to promoting their welfare, fostering academic excellence, and engaging in impactful social service.

Our mission extends beyond the classroom. Through cultural activities, literary forums, and community outreach, we aim to build character and nurture future leaders who contribute positively to society.

  • Student Welfare & Representation
  • Academic & Spiritual Growth
  • Social Service & Outreach

Diverse initiatives designed to foster knowledge, creativity, and community service.

Religious Studies
